- Lean project builds;
- blueprint web/pdf/DAG builds;
- final theorem target matches mathlib;
- Morgan--Tian theorem DAG source map complete;
- topology endgame stated under explicit interfaces;
- all pending theorems have source, owner, risk and replacement plan.

Deliverables:

- connected-sum API;
- fundamental group of connected sums;
- space-form and `S²`-bundle abstract models;
- extinction-to-classification induction proof;

Deliverables:

- curvature conventions and tensor APIs;
- Ricci flow definitions;
- maximum principles and Shi estimate statements;
- L-length and reduced volume definitions;
- reduced volume and noncollapsing statements reviewed by experts.

## 6--10 years: surgery and extinction replacement

Deliverables:

- surgery spacetime;
- controlled surgery;
- long-time existence;
- finite-time extinction;
- replacement of core interface theorems.
